The Golden Rules

Your Plan

🍚
No White Rice

Brown rice daily; basmati only occasionally. White rice spikes blood sugar — skip it.

🚫
No Sugar

No sugar in buttermilk, drinks, or anywhere. Natural fruit sugars are more than enough.

🫙
½ tbsp Oil Max

Half a tablespoon per meal. No deep frying — boil, roast, or steam only.

🌙
Soak Overnight

Oats and chia seeds must soak overnight for better absorption and gentler digestion.

🥚
Egg Whites Only

4 egg whites, no yolk. The white is pure protein; the yolk stays off the plate.

💧
3–4 Litres Water

Drink steadily all day. Detox water counts. Eat slowly; let every meal be a calm moment.
